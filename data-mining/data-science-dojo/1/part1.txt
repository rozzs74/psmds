1) What is Data? 
	-  Fundamental meaning, a data is collection of "objects" defined by "attributes".
		a) Attribute - is a "property or characteristic of an object".
			Example:
				1) Eye color of a person, temperature
				2) Variables, field, characteristic, feature, predictor etc.
		b) Collection  - "a collection of attributes" describe an object
			Example
				1) Other names record, point, case, sample, entity, instance etc.
